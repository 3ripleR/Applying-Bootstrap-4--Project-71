News About Environment

  • SJBHS Triathlon


    Test of Stamina and Talent SJBHS Triathlon, a PTA initiative was conducted on 24.11.2019. A total of 105 students from Stds 7 to 12 including 3 girls from ISC, participated in the triathlon. This triathlon comprised swimming (50 m), cycling (2 km) and running (150 m). The event began at 6 am with Father Sunil Fernandes offering a prayer. PTA Vice President Mr K C Joseph, members of PTA committee and around 30 to 40 volunteers were present for the event. The event began with std 10 and ISC and ended with Std 7.It was encouraging to see students responding and participating in the event. The event concluded at 8am and results are expected to be announced in December first week.

  • Annual Aquatic Meet


    Making a Splash! The Annual Aquatic Meet was held on 22nd November 2019 at SJBHS. This swimming gala is eagerly anticipated by the hard working and disciplined swimmers who have been training through the year to bring home laurels. The swimming pool was a buzz of activity: excited swimmers were in readiness, the judges were stationed at the starting mark and the expectant parents all ready to cheer their wards. The races began with a splash! Loud cheering accompanied the swimmers in their individual events; frenzied shouting assured the inter house participants that they were doing their best. Thunderous screaming resounded through the rafters of the indoor swimming pool during the interschool competitions! The chief guest was Olympian, Arjuna awardee and champion swimmer, Nisha Millet. The eminent swimmer encouraged the participants, asked them to push the boundaries to better their performances and to dream big to achieve their goals. Swimming is a water safety skill and needs to be perfected, she said.
